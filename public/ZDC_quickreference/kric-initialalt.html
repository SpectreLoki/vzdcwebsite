RICHMOND INITIAL ALTITUDES

Category Altitude
IFR (COLIN#, KALLI#, LUCYL#) climb via SID
IFR (READE#) 5,000
IFR (non-SID) 3,000
VFR (*) 2,500


(*) NOTE: VFR departures should receive only altitude, departure frequency, and squawk in their initial call. Aircraft performing closed traffic do not need an inital clearance call nor a squawk code, but may contact Ground ready for taxi.
