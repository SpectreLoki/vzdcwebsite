RALEIGH INITIAL ALTITUDES

Category Altitude
IFR (jets) 7,000
IFR (props) 2,000 (*)


(*) NOTE 1: SOP says that IFR prop initial altitude is 4,000, but as of AIRAC 2005 all departures which specify a prop altitude show it as 2,000.

NOTE 2: IFR departures not on a SID should be issued "... via fly runway heading, radar vectors {xxxxx}."

NOTE 3: SOP does not specify an altitude for VFR departures, but, my assumption is that it's 2,000. SOP does specify that aircraft should not receive a departure heading within their initial clearance; only "Maintain VFR at or below 2,000 until advised, departure frequency {xxx.x}, squawk {yyyy}." Aircraft performing closed traffic should receive a squawk code.
