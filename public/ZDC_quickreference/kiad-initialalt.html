DULLES INITIAL ALTITUDES

Category Altitude
IFR (all SIDs) 3,000
VFR (jets) 3,000
VFR (props) 2,500
VFR (heli) 1,300

NOTE 1: IFR departures from Dulles not able to accept a SID traffic are to be issued "... via fly runway heading."

NOTE 2: VFR departures from Dulles not cleared via a Heliroute nor for closed traffic are to be issued "... via fly runway heading."
