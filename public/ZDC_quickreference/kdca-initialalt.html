WASHINGTON INITIAL ALTITUDES

Category Altitude
IFR (all SIDs) 5,000
VFR (jets) 5,000
VFR (props) 2,500
VFR (heli) 1,300


NOTE: Washington IFR jet departures not on a SID are to be cleared "... via fly heading 320" if in North ops, or "... via fly heading 185" if in South ops. VFR departures and IFR props / turboprops should not be given a heading as part of their clearance.
