BALTIMORE INITIAL ALTITUDES

Category Altitude
IFR (CONLE#, FIXET#(*), TERPZ#) climb via SID
IFR (PALEO#, SWANN#, or no SID) 4,000
VFR (jets and turboprops) 3,500
VFR (helis and props) 2,000


(*) NOTE: the FIXET# Departure is ATC-assigned only; aircraft filing this should be re-assigned the TERPZ# Departure. For practical purposes, the FIXET# is not used on VATSIM.
