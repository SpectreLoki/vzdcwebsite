NORFOLK ATIS

Norfolk ATIS is recorded by voice:
"Norfolk International Airport information {ATIS ID},
{time} zulu, wind {direction} at {speed}, visibility {vis},
sky conditions [ clear | [ few clouds | scattered clouds | broken layer | overcast ] {x}{x} [ thousand | hundred ] ].
temperature {temp}, dew point {dew point}, altimeter {altimeter}.
ILS [ or visual ] approach runway {active rwy} in use;
advise on intial contact you have information {ATIS ID}."
