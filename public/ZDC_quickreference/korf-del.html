NORFOLK DEPARTURE GATES

NORFOLK INITIAL ALTITUDES

Category Altitude
IFR 4,000
VFR (*) 2,500


(*) NOTE: VFR departures should receive only altitude, departure frequency, and squawk in their initial call. Aircraft performing closed traffic do not need an inital clearance call, but may contact Ground ready for taxi. They should receive a squawk code with taxi instructions ("{callsign}, squawk {code}, runway {rwy}, taxi via {route}").
